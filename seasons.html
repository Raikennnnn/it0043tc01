Season 1

Season 1 introduces Franklin Saint, a young entrepreneur in Los Angeles during the early days of the crack cocaine epidemic. The season explores Franklin's rise in the drug trade, as well as the intersecting lives of CIA operative Teddy McDonald, Mexican wrestler Gustavo "El Oso" Zapata, and crime lord's daughter Lucia Villanueva.

Season 2

Season 2 delves deeper into Franklin's expanding drug empire. The stakes grow higher as Franklin's operation becomes more profitable and dangerous. Teddy continues his covert operations, while Gustavo and Lucia struggle with the internal power dynamics of their crime family.

Season 3

Season 3 highlights the intensification of the crack epidemic and its impact on the community. Franklin faces new challenges as law enforcement closes in, and personal relationships are strained. Teddy's operations grow riskier, and Gustavo and Lucia's criminal activities face new threats.

Season 4

Season 4 showcases Franklin's struggle to maintain control over his empire as rivals emerge and alliances shift. The season also explores the devastating effects of crack on the community and the increased law enforcement efforts to combat the epidemic.

Season 5

In Season 5, Franklin's empire is at its peak, but new threats loom on the horizon. The season focuses on the political and social ramifications of the crack epidemic, as well as the personal toll it takes on the characters.

Season 6

The final season, Season 6, brings the story to a gripping conclusion. The characters face their ultimate challenges as the consequences of their actions come full circle. The season wraps up the intertwined stories of Franklin, Teddy, Gustavo, and Lucia in a dramatic and impactful finale.

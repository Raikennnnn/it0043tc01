Blog Post: Hitting My Personal Record in Deadlift - 150kg

Published on July 13, 2024

In this blog post, I share my journey of hitting a personal record (PR) in deadlifting 150kg. I discuss the structured training program, the importance of proper nutrition and recovery, and the mental toughness required to achieve this goal. On the day of the lift, I successfully executed the 150kg deadlift, feeling a tremendous sense of accomplishment. This experience taught me valuable lessons about consistency, listening to my body, maintaining a positive mindset, and celebrating small wins. This achievement is a testament to the power of hard work and dedication.

Introduction
After months of intense training, dedication, and perseverance, I am thrilled to share that I have finally hit my personal record (PR) in deadlifting 150kg. This journey has been filled with highs and lows, but the sense of accomplishment I feel now makes it all worth it.

The Journey
Achieving this milestone wasn't easy. It required consistent effort, proper nutrition, and a well-structured workout plan. Here's a glimpse into the steps I took to reach this goal:

  • Setting a Goal: It all started with setting a clear, achievable goal. I knew I wanted to deadlift 150kg, and I set smaller milestones to track my progress.
  • Training Program: I followed a structured training program that focused on building strength and improving my deadlift form. This included variations of deadlifts, accessory exercises, and incorporating rest days for recovery.
  • Nutrition and Recovery: Proper nutrition played a crucial role in my journey. I focused on a balanced diet with adequate protein intake to support muscle growth and recovery. Additionally, I made sure to get enough sleep and rest to allow my body to recover.
  • Mental Toughness: Staying motivated and mentally strong was key. There were days when I felt like giving up, but I reminded myself of my goal and kept pushing forward.

The Big Day
On the day I attempted my PR, I felt a mix of excitement and nervousness. After a thorough warm-up, I loaded the barbell with 150kg. With every ounce of strength and determination, I approached the bar, took a deep breath, and executed the lift. The feeling of successfully lifting the weight was indescribable. It was a moment of pure triumph and joy.

Lessons Learned
This journey taught me several valuable lessons:

  • Consistency is Key: Regular and consistent training yields results.
  • Listen to Your Body: Understanding when to push and when to rest is crucial.
  • Stay Positive: A positive mindset can overcome many obstacles.
  • Celebrate Small Wins: Every small milestone is a step towards the bigger goal.

Conclusion
Hitting my PR in deadlifting 150kg is a testament to the power of hard work, dedication, and belief in oneself. I am proud of this achievement and excited to set new goals and continue my fitness journey. Remember, no goal is too big if you break it down into smaller, manageable steps and stay committed to your path.

Welcome to the World of Snowfall

Home

Snowfall is an American crime drama television series created by John Singleton, Eric Amadio, and Dave Andron. The series follows the early days of the crack cocaine epidemic in Los Angeles during the 1980s.

About

The story revolves around several characters whose lives are fated to intersect: Franklin Saint, a young street entrepreneur on a quest for power; Gustavo "El Oso" Zapata, a Mexican wrestler caught up in a power struggle within a crime family; CIA operative Teddy McDonald; and Lucia Villanueva, the daughter of a Mexican crime lord.

Characters

Meet the key players in Snowfall:

  • Franklin Saint: A young street entrepreneur.
  • Gustavo "El Oso" Zapata: A Mexican wrestler.
  • Teddy McDonald: A CIA operative.
  • Lucia Villanueva: The daughter of a Mexican crime lord.
  • Jerome Xavier Saint: is the uncle of Franklin Saint.
  • Leon “Lee” Simmons: is Franklin Saint's best friend and right hand man.
  • Einstein: is the brainiac and mathematician of Deon's Crip set.

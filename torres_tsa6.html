Welcome to the World of Snowfall

Home

Snowfall is an American crime drama television series created by John Singleton, Eric Amadio, and Dave Andron. The series follows the early days of the crack cocaine epidemic in Los Angeles during the 1980s. It explores the intertwined lives of various characters as they navigate through the burgeoning drug trade and its far-reaching impact on the community.

About

The story of Snowfall revolves around several characters whose lives are fated to intersect in the tumultuous backdrop of Los Angeles during the 1980s crack cocaine epidemic. Franklin Saint emerges as a young street entrepreneur driven by ambition and a desire for power in the drug trade. Gustavo "El Oso" Zapata, a Mexican wrestler with a complicated past, finds himself entangled in a power struggle within a crime family, navigating dangerous alliances and betrayals. CIA operative Teddy McDonald operates in the shadows, manipulating events to serve larger political agendas while confronting personal moral dilemmas. Lucia Villanueva, the daughter of a powerful Mexican crime lord, is determined to carve out her own path in the criminal underworld, facing challenges that test her loyalties and resilience.

Characters
